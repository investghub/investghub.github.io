Hi Hamster CEO folks, today I bring you how you can obtain the $HMSTR Deposit address and memo. This is very vital if you are to particpate in the upcoming Hamster Kombat TGE and airdrops. These airdrops will be undertaken on the Binance, OKX and Bybit exchanges.



So we assume that you fulfill the following requirements:

  • An active Hamster Kombat account. If not, you can create one here.

  • An active Binance account with KYC completed. If not, you can start the process here.

Hamster Kombat ($HMSTR) Listing Date

The Hamster Kombat ($HMSTR) token will be listed on popular cryptocurrency exchanges on 26th September, 2024. Along with the listing, there will be Binance Launchpool and Super Earn features available for the $HMSTR token. The Binance Launchpool token rewards will be 3.00% of the total supply of 3 billion tokens.

$HMSTR Deposit Address and Memo For Binance Exhange

In order to participate in the airdrops, you will need to obtain a $HMSTR Deposit address and memo from your preferred cryptocurrency exchange. I want to take you through how to abotain this address and memo.

  • Log in to your Binance account

  • Select Deposit option.


  • Select Deposit Crypto.


  • You will directed to Deposit Crypto page. Whilst there, type HMSTR in 'Select Coin' field.


  • In 'Select Network' field, Select TON. Go ahead to click on 'Ok, show me the address' button.


  • Your $HMSTR deposit address and memo will be revealed. You can also scan the QR codes provided to obtain same.


    • Well done! You are well set up to receive $HMSTR airdrops on Binance starting from 26th September 2024.
