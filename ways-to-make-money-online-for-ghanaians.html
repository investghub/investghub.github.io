Let's say you have a computer or a phone with internet connectivity and you are at loss as to how to make money with them, read on to know more. There are lots of ways to make money online. Its so much that many people get confused as to where to even start.

Before we dive into the how! Let us take a look at Attitudes and Mindsets required to successfully earn money online.

Attitudes & Mentalities For Earning Money Online

1. Growth Mindset:
Embrace learning and view challenges as opportunities to grow, adapt, and improve your skills.

2. Consistency and Discipline:
Commit to regular work routines and stay disciplined, even when results are not immediate.

3. Resilience and Persistence:
Be prepared to face setbacks and keep pushing forward without giving up.

4. Creativity and Innovation:
Think outside the box and explore new ways to solve problems or create value in the digital space.

5. Self-Motivation and Accountability:
Take charge of your progress, set clear goals, and hold yourself accountable for achieving them.

Now that you understand the required attitudes and mentalities for earning money online, here are the five best ways to earn money online for Ghanaians now.

1. Blogging

How much you can earn: GHS1000 - GHS3000 /month

This is for those with good ICT skills. You can earn money online through blogging. Blogging is an activity whereby content is created and published on a blog, which is a type of website or online platform where individuals or businesses share information, opinions, insights, or stories on various topics.

Blogs typically consist of regularly updated posts that allows readers to engage, comment, and share the content. making it both a personal and professional online activity.

Blogging like any other activity has some pros and cons. Pros include Creative Expression, Income Potential, Flexibility and Independence. Cons include Time-Consuming, Income is Not Guaranteed, Competition is High, and Technical Challenges.

How Do You earn Money with Blogging?

Bloggers can earn money through ads, sponsored posts, affiliate marketing, or selling products and services.

    • 2. Taking Online Surveys

    How much you can earn: GHS150 - GHS300 /month

    Taking surveys is another way to earn money online. This is one of the fastest and easiest ways of making money online. However, it is difficult since most surveys pay as little as little as GHS3-GHS5 per survey. Nevertheless, you can make it big if you are dedicated and consistent in taking surveys.

    Companies use online surveys to online surveys to collect valuable market research data to aid them create better products and services as well as offer more effective advertising campaigns. So you now know the reason why survey sites pay for your feedback.

    Online surveys have its pros and cons. Pros include Creative Expression, Income Potential, Flexibility and Independence. Cons include Time-Consuming, Income is Not Guaranteed, Competition is High and Technical Challenges.

    How Do You earn Money Taking Online Surveys?

    You can start earning through online surveys by following the steps below:
    • Identify Survey site
    • Register an account
    • Take available surveys to earn points/cash
    • Redeem points into cash
    • Withdraw money into preferred account after meeting withdrawal limit
